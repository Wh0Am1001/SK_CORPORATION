Services design, installation, repair and maintenance of power pipelines, power stations and high-voltage power transmission lines (115kV, 230kV) and renewable energy (sunlight, wind and biomass).

SK Corporation (BK Construction) is a Lao owned heavy equipment contractor established in Lao since 1988

SK Corporation (BK Construction) is a pre-qualified Lao government approved sub contractor and specializes in large Hydropower Dam construction.

SK Corporation (BK Construction) has 26 years of experience working on numerous roads, bridges, irrigation and many other civil engineering projects.

SK Corporation (BK Construction) offers a wide range of heavy equipment for lease and is a leading supplier of construction materials.

SK Corporation (BK Construction) is staffed with highly motivated and experienced personnel ready to handle any heavy equipment construction project from planning to completion.

SK Corporation (BK Construction) provides 24 hour emergency service on any type of projected in any weather.
